Get involved through an executive team, or one of our project teams!

Our executive teams help bring awesome events like hackshops, and monthly workshops to teach new skills such as Arduino and graphic design, and design challenges to campus.

Our project teams consist of four to seven people who build a project from the ground up for social good. They construct the projects we have featured here.



In April, we recruit project leads and executive members. In the fall, we recruit project team and committee members. Stay tuned for more updates on recruitment!
Contact us if you have questions or just want to chat at dfa-exec@mit.edu



Other Ways To Help Out


We are always looking for mentors, sponsors, and anyone willing to help! Send us an email at dfa-exec@mit.edu if you would like to help contribute. We would be happy to have you!
